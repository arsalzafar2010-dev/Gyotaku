Direct Method (Chokusetsu-ho):

1: Begin by painting sumi ink upon the tail of the fish, whilst gradually moving to the head.

2: Utilize the Halo effect. This can be performed by ensuring an empty space surrounding the pectoral fins, thus creating contrast.

3: Gently place thin side paper along the fish, followed by gently rubbing and placing pressure to display the details of the fish, before elevating the sheet of paper slowly.
